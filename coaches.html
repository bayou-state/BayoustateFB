Bayou State Coaching Staff

Building the War Tour Era

Head Coach Caleb Landry

Program builder and culture architect. Elevated Bayou State from a mid-tier BIG 12 program into a CFP contender during the 2032 War Tour season.

Offensive Coordinator Jameson Sheets

Designed the modern spread system that powered the 2032 offense. Emphasizes vertical passing and balanced run schemes.

Defensive Coordinator Earnest Hill

Hybrid 4-2-5 defensive architect. Known for aggressive pressure packages and developing elite pass rushers.

WR Coach / Graduate Assistant Cam Wake

Player development specialist. Played a major role in the emergence of young playmakers during the War Tour season.

OL Coach Marcus Benoit

Rebuilt the offensive line into one of the BIG 12’s most physical units.

RB Coach Tyrell Jackson

Focused on downhill, punishing run style that became a staple of Bayou State’s identity.

Strength & Conditioning – Coach Alonzo Pierce

Creator of the War Tour Performance Program.
